Kegg

Mobile iOS design for a Drexel Start-up

Overview

Role: Discovery, Branding, UX, UI Design

Having free time and nothing to fill it with can be all too familiar for college studens, which was a frustration that our client wished to address. They had the idea and technology for an application which would provide a private social network where college students could stay in the know about everything going on on campus. I provided branding and worked closely with the client to bring the product from a barebones prototype iOS app to a usable and well designed platform for social discovery. The application is now available in the App Store for download.
